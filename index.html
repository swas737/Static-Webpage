About Skills Connect

SkillsConnect aims to bring together colleges, students and companies and help them take the hiring process to the last mile. We aim to help talent acquisition be fast and we do this through

  • Digital ecosystem linking colleges & students to jobs and talent pool to companies
  • Helping students identify areas of growth and tools to upskill
  • Bringing allied services to this ecosystem at each stage

This platform is built with the flagship company of the Acura group which has been in the talent acquisition space for a decade. The company focuses on Turnkey Recruitment contracts, Talent Acquisition, Head Hunting, Executive Search, RPO services and Recruiter on Hire. Company has won various accolades in the field of Talent Acquisition and RPO services.
